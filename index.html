About Emerson 109

Located at the corner of Emerson and Ward in the heart of the McMaster student community and serving up fun times daily!

Chill and Relaxing

Stop by any time for a chill and relaxing atmosphere with team members that are friendly and knowledgeable.

Ready When You Are

Serving daily from 3pm and well into the night to 11pm due to Ontario Regulation 364/20, join us when it's convenient for you.

Walk-ins are welcomed, but reservations preferred by texting RESERVE to 289-426-2005.

Something For Everyone

With 10 taps, a variety of tall cans, and full stock of spirits, we're sure to have something to tickle your tastebuds!

Features

  • Conveniently located steps from a bike trail and on a bus route
  • Sunny patio from high noon to sun down
  • Available Wi-Fi for guests
